Submissions to the Journal of Kanha-Induced Cognitive Collapse (JKICC) must be made via email. Authors are required to submit their manuscripts in PDF format by sending them to the editorial team.

Manuscript Guidelines

Submissions should adhere to the following formatting and content guidelines:

  • File Format: PDF only.
  • Font & Formatting: Use a readable serif font (e.g., Times New Roman, 12 pt), 1.5 line spacing.
  • Length: No strict limit, but papers exceeding 20 pages should be justified in the cover letter.
  • Abstract: A brief summary (150-250 words) at the beginning of the manuscript.
  • Citations: Use a proper referencing style (APA, IEEE, or MLA).
  • Figures & Tables: Must be labeled and referenced in the text.
  • Review Process: All submissions undergo peer review to assess the depth of cognitive collapse induced by Kanha.
